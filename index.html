In a nutshell...

As an interdisciplinary group, the Interdisciplinary Network on Sympathy/Empathy and Imagination brings together scholarly research on the concepts of sympathy and empathy. We aim to show the continuity of ancient and contemporary thought about these concepts, and explore the relation of empathy and sympathy to the imagination.
