Sympathy, empathy and imagination

As an interdisciplinary group, the Interdisciplinary Network on Sympathy/Empathy and Imagination brings together scholarly research on the concepts of sympathy and empathy.

Aims

We aim to show the continuity of ancient and contemporary thought about these concepts, and explore the relation of empathy and sympathy to the imagination.

Resources

We bring together expertise and scholarship from various academic disciplines. Our resources page collects recent publications by members, and contains a bibliography with essential reading on sympathy, empathy and imagination.
